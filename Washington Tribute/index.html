George Washington

The first president of the United States of America

Some of the highlights of President Washington's life:

  • George Washington was born on February 22, 1732 in what is now the state of Virginia.
  • Washington served as Commander-in-Chief of the Continental Army during the 1775-1783 American War Revolutionary War.
  • He was sworn in as the first President of the United States on April 30th, 1789.
  • He would serve his country for a total of 8 years, leaving the offfice after his second term ended on March 4, 1797.
  • He nobly declined to run for a 3rd term in office eventhough he would have almost certainly won.
  • Washington did not want to die while in office, and wanted to set an example for presidents that would come after him.
  • George Washington died on December 18, 1799 at his home after coming down with an illnes and removing over half of his blood through deliberate bloodletting in an attempt to cure him of his illness.
  • He is interred at the Washington Family Tomb at Mount Vernon, Virginia.
